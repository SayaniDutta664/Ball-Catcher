HOW TO PLAY

Master the hand-controlled ball catcher game with this comprehensive guide. Learn the controls, mechanics, and strategies to achieve the highest score!

Game Features

Hand Tracking

Advanced computer vision technology tracks your hand movements in real-time for precise control.

Progressive Difficulty

The game gets faster and more challenging as you score points, keeping you engaged.

Global Leaderboard

Compete with players worldwide and track your progress with persistent high scores.

Game Instructions

Basic Controls

  • Position yourself about 3-5 feet from your camera for optimal hand detection
  • Ensure your hand is clearly visible in the camera frame with good lighting
  • Move your hand horizontally to control the basket position on screen
  • Keep your movements smooth and controlled for better accuracy
  • Use your entire arm for larger movements, not just your wrist

Game Objectives

  • Catch as many falling balls as possible to increase your score
  • Avoid missing balls - you can only miss 15 before game over
  • Reach higher levels by scoring points to increase difficulty
  • Beat your previous high score with each playthrough
  • Master the timing and movement to become a top player

Game Mechanics

Speed Increase

Every 10 points increases the ball falling speed, making the game progressively more challenging.

Level Progression

Advance through levels as you score points, with additional balls added at higher levels.

Score System

Each caught ball increases your score. Your high score is saved locally for future sessions.

Pro Tips

For best results, ensure you have adequate lighting and a plain background. Avoid wearing clothing that matches your skin tone, and keep your hand fully extended with fingers spread for optimal detection.

Tips & Strategies

Anticipate Ball Paths

Watch the trajectory of balls as they fall and position your basket in their path rather than chasing them.

Use Smooth Movements

Avoid jerky hand motions. Smooth, controlled movements will give you better precision when catching balls.

Focus on Center Balls

Prioritize catching balls that are falling in the center, as they're easier to reach from any position.

Manage Your Space

Position yourself so you have enough room to move your hand across the full width of the camera view.

Monitor Your Progress

Keep an eye on your score and misses. Knowing when you're close to leveling up helps you prepare for increased difficulty.

Adjust to Speed Changes

When the game speeds up, focus more on prediction than reaction. Anticipate where balls will be rather than where they are.

How It Works

1

Position Your Hand

Stand 3-5 feet from your camera with good lighting and ensure your hand is clearly visible in the frame.

Use your thumb and index finger to create a virtual catching bucket that the AI will track in real-time.

3

Track & Catch

Move your hand horizontally to follow the falling balls and position your bucket to catch them.
